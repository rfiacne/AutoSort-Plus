ℹ️ How AI Sorting Works

AutoSort+ uses AI to analyze your emails and automatically sort them into categories/folders based on their content. The AI will:

  • Read and understand email content
  • Identify key topics and themes
  • Match emails to appropriate categories/folders
  • Learn from your manual corrections to improve accuracy

📝 Custom Prompt

▶

Customize the prompt sent to AI for email classification.

Available placeholders:

  • {labels} — Your folder/label list
  • {subject} — Email subject line
  • {author} — Sender email address/name
  • {attachments} — Attachment filenames (comma-separated)
  • {body} — Email body content (recommended)
  • {email} — Email content (legacy, same as {body})

💡 Tip: Use {subject} and {attachments} for better classification accuracy.
